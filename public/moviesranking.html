The Best Movies According to Aditya

My top 5 movies of all-time.

Vertigo

A thriller you just cannot miss, Hithcock's evergreen classic.

Shawshank Redemption

A story of hope and friendship, one of best endings in cinema history.

Taxi Driver

A perfect portal of guy seeking validation from society amidst loneliness

The Notebook

A beautiful love story that will make you cry.

Scarface

A gangster movie that is both thrilling and entertaining, with a memorable performance by Al Pacino.
